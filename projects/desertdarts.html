About

Desert Darts is an endless android game inspired by a fervent desire to completely develop a mobile experience. Using the devices gyroscopic information, the player controls a target with the goal of landing precice hits in succession. The adaptive difficulty and complex dart pattern generation keeps this endless experience engaging.
