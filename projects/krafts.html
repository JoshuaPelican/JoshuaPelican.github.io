About

Krafts is an arts and crafts making experience made for a 48 hour game jam run by Game Makers Tool Kit (GMTK). With the theme of "Joined Together" I was inspired by glue and wanted to make a therapuetic experience reminiscent of kindergarten arts and crafts.

Players can arrange many different objects, glue them together, and even draw on them! After they finish they can shake off any un-glued objects and save a screenshot of their creation to their device. During the jam I angaged with the community by collecting they screenshots and putting them on the main game page!
