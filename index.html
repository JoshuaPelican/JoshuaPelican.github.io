Gameplay Programmer

Hello! I'm Joshua Pelkington, a gameplay programmer with 5+ years of game development and programming experience. I love the precision required for programming complex systems that work in tandem with art and design. My philosophy of developing beyond the ordinary has resulted in the projects I make now. Alongside this mindset, I take every opportunity to add a creative flair to the games I make.
